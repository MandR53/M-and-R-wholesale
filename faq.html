How do you source deals? ›

We combine targeted outreach, data filters, and partner referrals. We prioritize direct-to-seller when possible and disclose the chain when it’s not. Each lead includes what we know and what still needs verification.

  • Filters: zip code, price ranges, property type, rehab scope.
  • Signals: days vacant, distress markers, price deltas vs comps.
  • Verification: MLS/public comps, photos (when available), quick ARV logic.
How do you price / determine ARV? ›

We use nearby sold comps (distance/time filters), normalize by beds/baths/sqft/condition, and adjust for upgrades. For rentals, we cross-check predicted rents with area comps. Final ARV is a range with notes, not a single guess.

Deliverables: comp table or screenshots, ARV range, and quick notes on differences.

What does a “deal packet” include? ›

A standard packet typically contains:

  • Address & basics (beds/baths/sqft, year built, lot size)
  • Comps + ARV range
  • Photos/video link (when available)
  • Condition flags (roof/HVAC/plumbing/electrical)
  • Rough rehab notes or ranges
  • Timeline & next steps
Do you operate in both the US and Romania? ›

Yes. Our core activity is US wholesaling with processes and communication handled from Romania as needed. We also consider RO opportunities case-by-case (criteria, documentation, and timelines may differ).

What’s the typical timeline to close? ›

It depends on the path:

  • Acquisitions: 3–10 days from intake to decision-ready packet.
  • Assignments: 2–14 days to procure buyer interest (market dependent).
  • Title/Escrow: ~7–21 days for close after docs are in motion.

We share a target schedule and update it as milestones complete.

What documents do you use? ›

We rely on standard industry templates and adapt to your market norms. Common docs include:

  • Purchase agreement / assignment agreement
  • JV outlines (roles, splits, milestones)
  • Disclosures relevant to the deal

Note: Exact forms vary by state/county and title/escrow preferences.

How are payments and splits handled? ›

Payments are typically handled through title/escrow with clear invoices or assignment fees recorded on the closing statement. JV splits are defined in writing before any work starts.

Can you work with my buy box and criteria? ›

Yes. Send us your zip codes, budget, property types, bed/bath, rehab tolerance, exit strategy (flip/hold), and any must-avoid notes. We’ll align searches and only send what fits.

What if there’s an issue with the property or the paperwork? ›

We escalate fast. The Issues page outlines our approach: identify, disclose, propose fix, and track the resolution with clear timing. If a fix isn’t feasible, we don’t force the deal.
