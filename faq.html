FAQ

Do you publicly advertise properties?

No. We operate privately and only present opportunities under contract to vetted buyers.

How do I join your buyers list?

Go to Contact and send your buy box (markets, price, beds/baths, rehab level). We verify and add you.

What are typical terms?

As-is purchase, quick close preferred, proof of funds on request, non-refundable EMD to secure.

Do you work with agents and bird-dogs?

Yes. Bring closable leads; we pay fair referral fees. Agents welcome.
