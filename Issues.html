CATEGORIES

Common Edge Cases

Title & Liens

  • Unreleased mortgages, tax liens, HOA liens
  • Heirship/probate, missing deed links
  • Solution: clear with title; escalate timeline if third parties involved

Access & Condition

  • Tenants or occupants limiting access
  • No utilities for inspection
  • Solution: agent coordination, alternative time windows, utility-on requests

Paperwork & Signatures

  • ID mismatches, POA validation, out-of-country signers
  • E-sign acceptance by title/escrow
  • Solution: verify identity early; align form types with title preferences

Pricing & Comps

  • Outlier comps distorting ARV
  • Hidden upgrades/defects not visible in public photos
  • Solution: second-pass comp sanity check; adjust range & notes

Funds & Dates

  • EMD timing, proof of funds, closing scheduling conflicts
  • Solution: confirm feasibility before execution; buffer & milestone alerts

Compliance & Ethics

  • State-specific form requirements
  • Clear role disclosure (assignment/JV)
  • Solution: use standard templates; obtain written acknowledgements
PROCESS

How We Fix Problems

1 · Identify

Flag the issue

Capture what happened, where it blocks, and who’s needed to resolve. Add documents/screens where possible.

2 · Disclose

Share clearly

We notify affected parties with a short summary and the requested input/approval. No surprises.

3 · Propose

Offer fixes

Provide 1–3 options with timing impact and required docs/signoffs. Select the best path fast.

4 · Track

Own the timeline

Assign responsible parties, set a due date, and log updates until resolved or deal is cleanly declined.

ESCALATION

Escalation Ladder

Level 1 — Coordinator

  • Collects facts, updates the tracker
  • Contacts agent/title/seller/buyer as needed

Level 2 — Manager

  • Removes bottlenecks, adjusts schedule
  • Approves document changes / alternative paths

Level 3 — Principal

  • Final decision on proceed/decline
  • Direct outreach to stakeholders if needed

Legal / Compliance (as required)

  • State-specific forms and disclosures
  • Conflict checks and record updates
