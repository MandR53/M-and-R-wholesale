Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices and rights you have.

LEGAL

Last updated: October 19, 2025

1) Who We Are

“Wholesalers” provides information and coordination services around real estate wholesaling across the United States and Romania. If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at privacy@example.com.

2) What We Collect

We collect information you provide and data generated by your use of the site:

  • Contact details: name, email, phone, role (buyer/seller/agent/etc.).
  • Deal information: property basics, timelines, preferences.
  • Usage data: basic analytics (pages visited, approximate region/IP) and cookies to keep the site functional and improve it.
  • Files/attachments: if you send photos, spreadsheets, or documents.

3) How We Use Data

  • To respond to messages and provide requested services.
  • To match buy boxes with opportunities and coordinate partners.
  • To maintain security and prevent fraud/abuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce terms.
  • To improve the Site’s performance and content.

4) Lawful Bases (GDPR)

  • Contract: when processing is necessary to provide services you request.
  • Legitimate interests: e.g., security, service improvement, non-intrusive analytics.
  • Consent: for optional cookies/communications when required.
  • Legal obligation: when we must retain or disclose information.

5) Sharing & Transfers

  • Service providers/partners: title/escrow, agents, contractors, communication or analytics tools—only what’s necessary.
  • Legal: to comply with laws, protect rights, or respond to lawful requests.
  • International transfers: data may be processed in the US, EU, or other locations. We use appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs) where required.

6) Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for security and core functionality, and may use optional analytics cookies to understand site usage.

  • Essential: required for the site to work (cannot be switched off).
  • Analytics (optional): aggregated usage stats to improve the site.

You can control cookies via your browser settings. If we deploy a cookie banner, you’ll be able to opt in/out of non-essential cookies.

7) Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purpose collected, to comply with legal obligations, or to resolve disputes. Typical retention windows:

  • Contact/communication records: 24 months after last interaction (unless legally required longer).
  • Deal records/docs: up to 7 years for compliance and recordkeeping.
  • Analytics logs: 6–24 months in aggregated form.

8) Your Rights (GDPR/EEA/UK)

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your data.
  • Request correction (rectification) of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion (erasure) in certain circumstances.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Data portability (receive your data in a usable format).
  • Withdraw consent at any time (when processing is based on consent).
  • Lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

To exercise rights, email privacy@example.com. We may ask for information to verify your identity.

9) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is guaranteed 100% secure.

10) Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children. If you believe a child provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it where required.

11) Third-Party Links

The site may link to external websites we do not control. Their privacy practices apply to their sites.

12) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest version. Material changes will be highlighted where appropriate.

13) Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns? Email privacy@example.com. For general inquiries, use hello@example.com or the Contact page.

This Privacy Policy is informational and not legal advice. For specific obligations, consult your counsel.
